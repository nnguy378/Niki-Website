This entailed a lot of time dedicated after school to either choreograph dances, design invitations or posters, write scripts, direct assemblies, and sell tickets and merchandise. I also worked part-time at customer service jobs such as fast food chains and retail for about two years as a high school student. Through the program I was able to improve my public speaking and team management skills. I was also able to brush up on some of my other skills such as video editing and designing to market my group and I’s products during this program. These are some skills I picked up on that were self-taught from elementary school. I aspire to build upon these skills and acquire much more during my years in college before entering the workforce.
